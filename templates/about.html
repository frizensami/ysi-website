Young Sustainable Impact is...

Young Sustainable Impact was started in Oslo, Norway by a group of youths passionate about bringing young people around the world together to tackle sustainability problems, based around the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals .


They saw a lack of start-ups in the field of sustainable development aimed at alleviating bigger world problems, and decided to bridge the gap between idea generation and impact, as well as engage youth in sustainability and entrepreneurship.


In 2017, with an overwhelming response of 10,053 applicants from 170 countries , YSI Global gathered 25 amazing participants and expanded to 5 new countries within 12 months.


YSI Southeast Asia has its operations based in Singapore, and will head the programme for Southeast Asian youths.
